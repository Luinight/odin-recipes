Cannoli

Description

a dessert to die for

Ingredients

  • Cannoli Shells
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Confectioners Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Fine-Mesh Strainer
  • Large Piping Bag

Steps

  1. Place the ricotta cheese into a fine mesh strainer and place it in the fridge to drain for at least 6 hours and up to 24 hours.
  2. In a large bowl combine the drained ricotta cheese, confectioners sugar, 1/4 cup of the mini chocolate chips, vanilla extract, and orange zest (if using);
  3. Pipe the filling into one end of cannoli shell
  4. Place remaining mini chocolate chips on a small plate. Dip each end of the cannoli into the chocolate chips
